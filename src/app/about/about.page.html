Dota Lore

        Dota Lore is an app to explore the vast number of hereos existed in the video game called Dota 2. Dota 2 is the deepest and most complex moba game ever made where there's always a new strategy to discover. This app aims to help the users to capture a fraction of the lore and interesting information of ever expanding universe of Dota 2.
